Welcome To Kerala Vishwakarma Sabha

The VISWAKARMMAS are believed to be the decedents of the Creator of the Universe,LORD VISWABRAHMA . In “ Mundakopanishad ” Manthra : "Bhrahma Devanaam Pradhama Samba Bhuva" promulgates the non-existence of any other geneology before the Lord Bhrahma.VEDAS are unquestionably regarded as the oldest literary composition in any language in the world wherein RIGVEDA is the foremost among all. The word VISWAKARMMA was firstly used in Rigveda to depict Lord Viswabrahma as the Creator of the Universe in its 80 and 81 SUKTAS in the 10th MANDALA.

Our Vission

In the process of time, Viswakarmas known after the name the Creator of Universe owing to their skillful creative contribution to the entire human race. At the outset they supplied the essentials needed in the making of basic human necessities such as food, clothe and shelter. Their constructive benefaction in the advent of cultural development in later stages of human evolution was very significant. Their adroit gifts for the development of art, literature, music and spirituality were fabulous than their own grandeur in agricultural, industrial and tertiary sector. But in the later stages of evolution the community gradually lost their extensive predominance in the socio- economic and cultural spheres due to socio-economic transition. Thus it becomes the primary organizational vision of SABHA to regain the lost majesty of Viswakarmmas to benefit the entire humanity.

Our Mission

The past glory of the country subsists in her marvellous colossal fabrics. But even modern science fail to unveil the mystery behind their design, engineering and craftsmanship either to maintain or to reconstruct them for the posterity without losing their ingenuity. Many such environmental-friendly wonders now face irrecoverable ruin. All the creations of Viswakarmas were based on the principle of Preservation of Mother Nature. Hence the mission of Sabha is to bring back the magnificence of Country's exquisite past through the development of reverse engineering .

Our Goal

Sabha aims to equip a future generaton having a Bharatheeya educational,cultural and ethical vision.Today the new generation transcends an appalling socio-cultural and economical arena amidst terrorism,narcotism and sedition. Neverthless the country needs a posterity with high scientific temperament containing pure patriotic insight. Such a generation can only elevate patriotic intellectuals who would stand up for the country and the Universe as well to recite the impressive world famous slogan " Loka Samastha Sukhino Bhavanthu". So in the making of a New glorified lndia Sabha owes to bestow our part.

Abouts
